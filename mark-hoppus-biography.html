Who is Mark Hoppus?

Mark Hoppus is an American musician, singer, songwriter plus record producer.

He is known as the bassist and co-lead vocalist for the band Blink-182.

Mark Hoppus: Bio, Birth, Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity, Education

Mark Hoppus was born Mark Allan Hoppus on 15 March 1972, in  California, USA.

He holds a mixed ethnicity of Finnish, English, German, Dutch/Frisian, Welsh, Irish, and French.

His parents were Kerry Wernz (mother) and Tex Hoppus (father). Her younger sister’s name is Anne Hoppus.

He studied at Annandale High School. He used to study at Burroughs High School but had to leave school due to an issue.

Mark Hoppus: Professional Life, Career

In 1995, Mark Hoppus’ band Blink-182 recorded their first album called  Cheshire Cat.

In 1995, the band signed a contract for a national tour it lasted till the East Coast.

They performed with Unwritten Law, Sprung Monkey, and 7 Seconds.

The band owned their own van. MCA Records signed their band and co-distributes their next release.

After getting a huge response from the crowd, the band was popular all over the united states.

In 2013 Feb, he performed with his band in Australia. In 2015, they performed in California.

Recent Albums

Recently in the years 2016 and 2019, this talented singer released his two albums.

They were California and Nine respectively.

Mark’s other music albums are Take Off Your Pants and Jacket (2001), Untitled Blink-182 album (2003), Neighborhoods (2011), and Dogs Eating Dogs EP (2012).
