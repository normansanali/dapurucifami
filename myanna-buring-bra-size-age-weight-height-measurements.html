MyAnna Buring Bra Size, Age, Weight, Height, Measurements

Biography:

MyAnna Buring was born on September 22, 1979 in Sweden. She attended the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art where she graduated in 2004. She made her television debut in an episode of the series Murder Prevention.

In 2005, she starred as Sam in the horror film The Descent, which marked her film debut. She has since starred in several other movies including The Omen, Grindhouse, Doomsday, Credo and many others.

She gained worldwide recognition in 2011 after being cast as Tanya in The Twilight Saga: Breaking Dawn Part 1, a role she reprised in The Twilight Saga: Breaking Dawn Part 2.
