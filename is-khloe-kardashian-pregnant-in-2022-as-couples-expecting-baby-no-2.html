Is Khloe Kardashian Pregnant In 2022 As Couples Expecting Baby No 2? The Talks Today

According to TMZ, Khloe Kardashian is expecting her second child with Tristan Thompson, but she won’t be expecting in 2022.

The reality star’s on-again, off-again lover and her recently divorced. If stories are to be believed, the couple’s family will grow even though they aren’t back together (again).

True, their four-year-old daughter, is already part of their family.

Couples expecting their second child via surrogate

Khloe hasn’t officially announced anything, but according to “several sources” cited by TMZ, the baby will be delivered through a surrogate.

A second insider reportedly stated that the baby “may already have been born in the last day or so” while one source told the outlet that the birth is approaching and the diva might receive her bundle of joy in a few days.

The revelation was apparently confirmed by a Kardashian representative, who reportedly told Page Six: “We can confirm True will have a sibling who was conceived in November. Khloé is immensely appreciative of the exceptional surrogate for this wonderful blessing.

Khloe Kardashian is not conceiving again

Fans had thought that Khloe will become pregnant again in 2022 since the news of her and Tristan’s second child surfaced.

She has chosen surrogacy this time, thus it is known for sure that she is not pregnant.

Additionally, she hasn’t displayed any pregnancy-related symptoms. In all of her most recent Instagram photos, she can be seen sporting a toned physique.

After giving birth to True in April 2018, Khloe became a mother for the first time. Tristan attended the birth of their first child.

An examination of her bond with Tristan Thompson

In August 2016, Khloe and Tristan first fueled dating rumors. In the same year, they made a combined appearance at Flo Rida’s birthday celebration in Miami, which appeared to confirm their connection.

They supposedly split up in 2019, but the basketball star became embroiled in cheating allegations in the years that followed.

To give their love another chance, they got back together in August 2020. However, less than a year later, they broke up once more.
