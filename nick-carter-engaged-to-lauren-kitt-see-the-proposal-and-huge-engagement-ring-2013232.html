Nick Carter Engaged to Lauren Kitt: See the Proposal and Huge Engagement Ring!

Lucky Lauren! Nick Carter planned the perfect proposal for girlfriend Lauren Kitt on Feb. 20.

With the ocean in the background and their dog at their feet, the shirtless star got down on one knee and pulled out a ring from his swim trunks. Kitt, wearing a red bikini, gave her future husband a big hug after accepting his proposal.
