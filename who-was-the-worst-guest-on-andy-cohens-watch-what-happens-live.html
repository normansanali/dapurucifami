Who was the worst guest on Andy Cohen's Watch What Happens Live?

Andy Cohen has hosted a number of reality TV stars and A-list celebrities on Watch What Happens Live. Many times, they end up spilling some major T. Other times, their anecdotes make the mainstream news. However, Cohen has had some major duds on his talk show. He even admitted that Amber Rose was one of his worst guests since she refused to cooperate during the Plead The Fifth game. Other celebrities who have had their Watch What Happens Live interviews bashed include Brandi Glanville and Debra Winger.
