Jeffrey Rebish- Biography

Jeffrey Rebish is a board-certified dermatologist, entrepreneur, and member of the American Academy of Dermatology and the American Society for Dermatologic Surgery. He is most known as the spouse of Sandra Lee, often known as “Dr. Pimple Popper.”

Jeffrey Rebish- Birth, Age, Ethnicity, Siblings, Education

Jeffrey Rebish was born on November 25, 1972, in New York State, USA. He is Harvey and Hafdis Rebish’s son, as well as Stuart and Karen Rebish’s siblings. He grew up in Westchester County with his two brothers and sisters. His parents were both professors at a prestigious medical school.

As a result of his interest in medicine, Rebish enrolled as an undergraduate at Dartmouth College in Hanover, New Hampshire, shortly after graduating from a nearby high school. Following graduation, he enrolled in medical school at Hahnemann University School of Medicine in Philadelphia, PA, where he finished with a degree in dermatology in 1998. Jeffrey completed his internship in San Bernardino County Hospital in San Bernardino, CA, as well as Allegheny General Hospital in Pittsburgh, PA. He earned a master’s in Dermatology from New York Medical College, where he also finished his residency and his medical degree from Drexel University College of Medicine.

Jeffrey Rebish- Relationship, Married Life
