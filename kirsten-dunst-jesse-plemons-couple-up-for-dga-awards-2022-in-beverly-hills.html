Kirsten Dunst &amp; Jesse Plemons Couple Up for DGA Awards 2022 in Beverly Hills

The 39-year-old actress and the 33-year-old actor posed together at the 2022 DGA Awards on Saturday evening (March 12) in Beverly Hills, Calif.

For the event, Kirsten looked pretty in a black, velvet blazer over a white dress while Jesse sported a brown suit.

Other stars in attendance included Kirsten and Jesse‘s The Power of the Dog director Jane Campion, Maggie Gyllenhaal and husband Peter Sarsgaard, Judd Apatow, Nomadland director Chloe Zhao, Dune director Denis Villeneuve and wife Tanya Lapointe, comedian Bo Burnham, Obi-Wan Kenobi actor Joel Edgerton, Moonlight director Barry Jenkins, and Netflix CEO Ted Sarandos.
