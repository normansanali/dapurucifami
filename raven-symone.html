Raven Symone

Raven Symone is an actress, singer-songwriter, and model from the United States who is best known for her appearances in Dr. Dolittle and College Road Trip. She has been on The Cosby Show, Hanging with Mr. Cooper, That’s So Raven, and Big City Greens, among other shows.

Raven was born on December 10, 1985, in Atlanta, Georgia, United States. She is of African-American ancestry and of American nationality. Sagittarius is her zodiac sign.

Net Worth, Salary, and Income

Raven Symone began her career at the age of two. She worked for Atlanta’s Young Faces Inc. It was then on to Jello, Fisher Price and Ritz Crackers advertisements. When she was four, she appeared on The Cosby Show. Following that, she starred in shows like Hanging with Mr. Cooper and Kim Possible. In 2003, she starred in the TV series That’s So Raven as Raven Baxter. The series earned her BET, Young Artist, and Kid’s Choice nominations. At the age of seven, she signed with MCA Records. It was released on June 22, 1993, and featured songs like Raven is the Flavor and That’s What Little Girls Are Made Of. Her albums include Undeniable and Raven-Symone.

She starred in the Disney film The Cheetah Girls in 2003 and later in its sequel, as well as providing the soundtrack for both films. She has also appeared in films such as Dr. Dolittle, College Road Trip, and many others. She has a net worth of roughly $55 million as a result of her various endeavors.

Partner, Spouse, or Kids?

Raven has had a few boyfriend/girlfriend relationships in the past. She dated actor Orlando at first, but the date of their relationship is unknown. After meeting on the sets of That’s So Raven in October 2003, she fell in love with Jonathan ‘Lil J’ McDaniel. The couple, however, called it quits in November 2007. She moved on with Brazilian actor Jussie Smollett just a month after their breakup. In July 2011, she also ended her romance, this time with AzMarie Livingston, an American model. She dated her for almost four years before splitting up in October 2015.

She hasn’t been linked to anyone who could be considered her boyfriend/girlfriend since then.
