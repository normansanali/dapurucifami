Dr. Mark Jones, MD Covington, LA

Overview of Dr. Jones

Dr. Mark Jones is a general surgeon in Covington, LA and is affiliated with multiple hospitals in the area, including St. Tammany Health System and Our Lady of the Angels Hospital. He received his medical degree from LSU School of Medicine in Shreveport and has been in practice 8 years. He is one of 15 doctors at St. Tammany Health System and one of 8 doctors at Our Lady of the Angels Hospital who specialize in General Surgery. He has 3 publications.
