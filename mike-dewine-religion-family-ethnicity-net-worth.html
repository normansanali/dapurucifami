Many people search for Mike Dewine Religion as he currently serves as Ohio’s 70th and current governor. He is an American politician and attorney.

Richard Michael Dewine, the 70th and current governor of Ohio State, was born on January 5, 1947, and is a Republican Party member.

Dewine embarked on his journey in American politics serving as a county prosecutor for four years and then being elected to the Ohio State Senate in 1980.

He was a U.S. representative from 1983 to 1991 and the 59th lieutenant governor of Ohio under George Voinovich from 1991 to 1994.

Furthermore, from 1995 to 2007, Mike was a U.S. senator. From 2011 to 2019, he was the 50th attorney general of Ohio.

With 50.4% of the vote, Dewine defeated Democrat Richard Cordray to win the governorship for the first time in 2018.

Likewise, in 2022, he easily defeated Democratic candidate Nan Whaley and won reelection.
