Malin Akerman Dating Colin Egglesfield, She Worries He's &quot;Too Hot&quot;

Trophy girlfriend? Malin Akerman and Colin Egglesfield are officially dating, a source confirms to Us Weekly. The pair have kept mum about their relationship since first being photographed together in Beverly Hills earlier this month.

"They're taking it slow," a source tells Us. In quite the head-scratcher, a pal adds: "She thinks he's almost too hot for her!"

The Trophy Wife actress, 35, and Egglesfield, 41, were first spotted enjoying a low-key evening at Sugarfish Sushi for dinner on April 1.

On April 12, the pair continued their new love fest at the 2014 Toyota Pro/Celebrity Race in Long Beach, Calif. Akerman dressed casually in jeans and a black T-shirt as she cheered on the Client List alum.
