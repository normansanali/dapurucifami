Kanye West Reportedly Shot Music Video For &quot;Waves&quot; In Scotland

Kanye West's The Life of Pablo is finally available to stream beyond just TIDAL, and not only that, it's begun charting on the Billboard 200 (at #1 no less, earning him his seventh consecutive #1 album). While all this is happening, Kanye's set his sights on a new music video-- we haven't received one yet from TLOP, and it's unclear if there are any more visuals in the bag.

Ye reportedly went with "Waves" for a video, perhaps indicating that the record will be pushed as an official single. Kanye made a trip to the Isle of Skye in Scotland for the video shoot, with a Scottish publication, Daily Record, reporting on his stay there. According to their report, Kanye and his team stayed at the Skeabost House Hotel, where they occupied half of the hotel's fourteen rooms. "Kanye was the perfect guest - charming, courteous and polite," one of the hotel owners said, "We were delighted to offer him and his companions our usual Highland hospitality and would welcome him back any time."

A spokesman for the hotel then went into detail about Kanye's stay, although details about the video shoot itself are extremely slim. Still, the spokesman revealed, "It was all very exciting. The reservation, which included one room for Kanye, then one for his stylist and others for the video team had been booked out a few days in advance." He continued, "The staff at the hotel say he chatted to everyone. Photographs were banned. But they said he was just like one of the guys really. They did ask if Kim Kardashian West was part of his entourage, but they were told she was elsewhere. He was very busy and didnâ€™t have a lot of time. He was definitely here to work."
