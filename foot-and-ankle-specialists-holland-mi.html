Foot and Ankle Specialists - Holland MI

Our podiatrists understand that you are a unique individual with a complex medical history. We won’t treat you like another patient we will treat you like a member of the family.

Whether you are looking for care for you, your child, or another loved one, we are committed to treating your foot, heel, and ankle pain.

At Foot and Ankle Specialists, our philosophy is consultative medicine. This means we want doctor and patient to work together as a team. We involve patients at every step of their care and take their needs into account. We care about you not just your feet.

Our podiatrists in Holland, MI are trained to treat a wide variety of podiatric conditions. These include but are not limited to arthritis, bunions, corns and calluses, flat feet, heel pain, hammertoe and claw toe, ingrown toenails, toenail fungus, neuromas and all manner of sports-related foot and ankle injuries. After we have your condition under control, we will work with you on lifestyle changes to make sure your pain stays away for years to come.

And if you are seeking treatment for your child, our podiatrists are experienced in pediatric podiatry. So they can help your child with issues like toe walking, in-toeing and out-toeing, frequent falls and other abnormal gait issues.

In addition to consultative medicine, we at Foot and Ankle Specialists value evidence-based medicine and scientific research. This is why many of our podiatrists have contributed to research studies to advance the field. Our podiatrists at Holland, MI also keep their board certifications fully up-to-date.

Despite their advanced surgical training, our podiatrists will choose conservative, nonsurgical treatments whenever possible. This is because surgery is a last resort for us. We want to relieve your foot, heel, and ankle pain. So if this is possible to do without a long, painful recovery period from surgery, we will make it happen.

Our podiatry practice in Holland, MI is well-equipped with amenities to make your visit as comfortable and convenient as possible. We offer coat check, air conditioning and Wi-Fi. And our office is also completely accessible by wheelchair. We try to focus on your entire experience with Foot and Ankle Specialists, not just the medical care. We want you to feel at ease during your visit from beginning to end.

Call now or click the Appointment button on this page to start your Foot and Ankle Specialists journey. We never want a logistical barrier to interfere with our patients’ ability to access the care they need, so we offer online appointments through your computer or smartphone and accept most major insurance plans.
