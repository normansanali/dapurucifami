Paige DeSorbo News - Us Weekly

Name:Paige DeSorbo

Hometown:New York, NY

Birthday:November 6, 1992

About

Paige DeSorbo joined Bravo’s Summer House in 2019 during season 3. She went on to appear in the Southern Charm and Summer House crossover, Winter House, in 2021.

The TV host and journalist writes about fashion for Betches Media. She also cohosts the “Giggly Squad” podcast with Summer House costar and comedian Hannah Berner.

DeSorbo dated Perry Rahbar throughout season 4 of Summer House before they called it quits in October 2020. She sparked a romance with Southern Charm alum Craig Conover the following year.
