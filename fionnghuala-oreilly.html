Fionnghuala OReilly

Birth Name: Fionnghuala Isibeal Tome O’Reilly

Place of Birth: Fort Knox, Hardin, Kentucky, United States

Date of Birth: August 20, 1993

Ethnicity:
*Irish (father)
*African-American (mother)

Fionnghuala O’Reilly is an Irish-American actress, model, television presenter, engineer, and beauty pageant titleholder. She was crowned Miss Universe Ireland 2019.

She was born in Fort Knox, Kentucky, and raised in the U.S. and Swords, Ireland. Her father, Fergal, is Irish. Her mother, Vanetta, is African-American, from San Francisco.

Fionnghuala’s paternal grandfather was named Thomas O’Reilly.

Fionnghuala’s paternal grandmother was named Isabella.

Fionnghuala’s maternal grandmother was named Martha Juanita Eley.
