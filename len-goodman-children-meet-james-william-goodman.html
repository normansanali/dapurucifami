Len Goodman Children: Meet James William Goodman

Len Goodman was a well-known figure in the world of ballroom dancing and entertainment. He was born on April 25, 1944, in London, England, and grew up in a working-class family.

Goodman started dancing at a young age, and by the time he was in his twenties, he had become a professional ballroom dancer.

Over the years, Goodman has won numerous dance championships and has become one of the most respected and sought-after dance teachers in the world.

He has also served as a judge on various dance competitions, including the popular TV show “Dancing with the Stars” in the US and “Strictly Come Dancing” in the UK.

Goodman is known for his expertise in ballroom dancing and his ability to provide constructive criticism to dancers without being harsh or overly critical.

He has also been praised for his charming personality and his ability to connect with audiences and contestants alike.

Aside from his work in the dance world, Goodman has also appeared in various TV shows and films. He has hosted his own TV show, “Len Goodman’s Partners in Rhyme,” and has made guest appearances on popular TV shows such as “Friends” and “Absolutely Fabulous.”

Len Goodman died on April 22, 2023, in Royal Tunbridge Wells, United Kingdom.
