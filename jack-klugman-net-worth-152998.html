Jack Klugman Net Worth

Jack Klugman was a three-time Emmy award-winning actor born in Philadelphia, PA on April 27, 1922. He was best known for his roles as Oscar Madison on TV's The Odd Couple and as the title character in Quincy, M.E. for eight years. He was a beloved actor who left a lasting legacy in the entertainment industry.

About

Three time Emmy award winner who portrayed the role of Oscar Madison on TV's The Odd Couple and starred as the eponymous character in Quincy, M.E. for eight years.

Before Fame

He roomed with fellow star Charlie Bronson as a struggling actor in New York after serving in the Army during WWII.

Trivia

Family Life

He married Brett Somers in 1953 and they had two children before divorcing.
