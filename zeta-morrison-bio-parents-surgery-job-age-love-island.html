Meet Love Island's Zeta Morrison! Bio, Parents, Surgery, Job, Age

If confidence had a face, it would be that of Zeta Morrison. She wasn’t on Love Island to play games. Claiming she had zero “toxic traits,” Zeta was confident she wouldn’t go home single.

So, who is this bold and beautiful lady? Get to know her better as this Zeta Morrison Bio proceeds. 

Zeta Morrison On Love Island

Love Island USA season 4 made the summer of 2022 even hotter by bringing together a brand new set of sexy singles and mashing them together in a gorgeous villa as they searched for love. Turing the heat on, the islanders also were sent to compete in “naughtier games and steamier challenges,” as they decided if they wanted to stay with their current partner or “recouple” with someone new.

And obviously, those who lacked connections were dumped from the island.

Our star, Zeta joined the show to make a genuine connection. She wanted someone honest, generous, very sweet, kind-hearted, who loves to listen to her, and very very sexy. Infact, she made it clear before entering the villa that she was there to find her husband and she was super serious about it!

In the past, Zeta dated “so many different kinds of men,” with one even being a famous person. It was all great at the start, but then it wasn’t. So, not wanting to repeat history, Zeta was careful about who she dated on Love Island. 

But with that being said, Zeta did go for someone she is sexually attracted to. After all, this is Love Island.

Joining Zeta this season were her co-stars — Andy Voyen, Courtney Boerner, Felipe Gomes, Deborah Chubb, Isaiah Campbell, Sereniti Springs, Jesse Bray, Sydney Paight, and Timmy Pandolfi.

Fun Fact: Zeta can fluently speak the Ghanaian dialect.

Zeta Morrison Job

Zeta Morrison is a model and the owner of a Jewelry Line called Z-Chain, incorporated in May 2015.

Over her decade of a career in modeling, she’s modeled for brands like BodyFab, and Lounge, and graced the runway of Next in Fashion in 2022 for Kiki & Farai. 

Someday, she hopes to make it to Victoria’s Secret. “But I got a long way to go. I’m a down-to-earth girl, and photoshoots with me would never be boring,” she added.

Also, Zeta has appeared on the TV show Crime and Justice (2022) as “Sebu Armstrong” and in the movie Dutch (2021) as “London.”

After filming Crime and Justice, Zeta took it to his IG to share a promo clip captioned, “The time has come. I had the most amazing time on this set with the most amazing cast/crew. Really thanking God above for this one.”

For those who don’t know, Zeta has 6 years of acting study under her belt. Also, she received her associate’s degree in psychology from Los Angeles City College and her BA in Clinical Child Psychology from California State University, both in 2013.

No wonder, she takes pride to call herself a professional babysitter. And meanwhile, she’s babysitting, she likes to write and she even was working on an autobiography at the time of writing this article.
