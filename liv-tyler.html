Liv Tyler is an American actress, producer, voiceover artist, singer, and former model who is best known for playing Arwen Undómiel in the Lord of the Rings film trilogy. In addition to that, she has also been cast in projects like The Incredible Hulk, Stealing Beauty, The Leftovers, 9-1-1: Lone Star, The Strangers, Harlots, Gunpowder, Inventing the Abbotts, One Night at McCool’s, Onegin, Jamie Marks Is Dead, The Ledge, Jersey Girl, Dr. T & the Women, Plunkett & Macleane, That Thing You Do!, Armageddon, Reign Over Me, Space Station 76, and Robot & Frank. Moreover, Liv Tyler has served as a UNICEF Goodwill Ambassador for the United States and as a spokesperson for Givenchy‘s line of perfume and cosmetics.
