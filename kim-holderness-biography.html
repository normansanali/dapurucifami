Kim Holderness Bio (Wiki)

Who is Kim Holderness?

Kim Holderness is an American actor, TV journalist, Youtuber, and businessperson.

She is the CEO and Founder of Holderness Family Productions which creates content like family-centered parodies, skits, and vlogs.

She is also a blogger, podcast host, and entertainer.

Kim Holderness- Job & Career

To begin with, Kim first started as a WKMG TV for two years starting in 2002.

Next, she was The WPEC-News12 reporter from Nov 2000 to Nov 2002.

Later, she was a reporter from September 2004-2008, four years in Inside Edition.

In the year 2011-2012, she worked in video production at the LStar venture until when she had encompassed industrial knowledge like Radio Broadcasting, News Writing, Media Production, Creative Writing, Multimedia, Breaking News, Entertainment, Content Strategy, Web Video, Post Production, HD Video.
