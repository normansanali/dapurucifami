Jackson Hurst Marries Stacy Stas: Drop Dead Diva Wedding Details

Grayson Kent is taken! Jackson Hurst and his actress fiancee Stacy Stas tied the knot in San Juan Capistrano, Calif., on Friday, June 6, a rep for the Drop Dead Diva actor confirms exclusively to Us Weekly.

Hurst, 35, and his new bride were feted by close family and friends for the special occasion, which was planned by Gather Events. Stas wore a Monique Lhuillier gown and a handmade head piece by Janie Baltzer, with hair and makeup by 1011 Makeup. She was escorted by her father and was joined by her sisters as her maids of honors, who carried flowers by Krista Jon. Jackson and his groomsmen donned Vera Wang suits.

The newlyweds tearfully recited their own vows. After saying "I do," they walked down the aisle to Indiana Jones' "Raiders March." The theme of the wedding was an old New Orleans vibe with a storybook twist. Gather Events created a vintage-inspired French parasol installation over the cocktail reception, and painted old books with the table numbers.

"I've never been more excited for any other day in my life than I was for my wedding day," Jackson tells Us.
