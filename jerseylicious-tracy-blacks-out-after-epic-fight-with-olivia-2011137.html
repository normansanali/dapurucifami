Jerseylicious' Tracy Blacks Out After Epic Fight With Olivia

It's a knock down, drag out Jersey girlfight!

Jerseylicious' Tracy DiMarco and Olivia Blois Sharpe have had their fair share of massive blowouts over the years during their Style series, but a spat on Sunday's episode — where the girls come to blows over a boyfriend they have in common — just may top them all.

"It's the most dramatic scene you'll see this season," DiMarco hints to Us Weekly of the bar fight where the girls physically attack one another. "It gets really intense, pretty much over nothing, but the last thing you'd ever think happens happens."

As the above preview clip shows, all hell breaks loose when DiMarco gets close to Mikey, an ex-boyfriend who just happens to be Blois Sharpe's current squeeze.

"Don't touch my boyfriend, bitch!" Blois Sharpe snaps as DiMarco hits the floor, thinking a bouncer hit her but not realizing it was actually her nemesis who kicked her in the head.
