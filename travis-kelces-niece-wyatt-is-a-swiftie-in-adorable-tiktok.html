Travis Kelce's niece Wyatt is a Swiftie in adorable TikTok

Explore More

We can’t shake off this cuteness.

Jason Kelce’s 4-year-old daughter, Wyatt, is confirmed to be a fan of Uncle Travis Kelce’s rumored girlfriend Taylor Swift.

In an adorable TikTok posted Monday by Jason’s wife, Kylie, Wyatt sits on the couch and attempts to learn the names of her dad’s Philadelphia Eagles teammates.

“Just another Swiftie tryna learn football, or a kid tryna avoid a nap. Who knows?” Kylie, 31, captioned the clip, referencing the “Anti-Hero” singer’s dedicated legion of fans.

“Good job Wyatt!,” the official Eagles account clapped.

Fellow Swifties and therefore newly-devoted football fans echoed the Eagles’ excitement.

“THE CAPTION!!!! ugh I need a Kylie/Baby Wy/TSwift meet and greet so bad,” one fan begged.

“I think Kylie and Wy need their own podcast! I adore Jason and Travis but way more interested listening to the girls commentary,” another requested.

“So cute… New Heights needs a Baby Wy segment!” a third wrote, mentioning the Kelce brothers’ podcast.

Swift, 33, did not attend Sunday’s Kansas City Chiefs win against the Minnesota Vikings despite going viral as a spectator the two prior weeks.

On Friday, Kelce’s mom, Donna, weighed in on the NFL capitalizing off her younger son’s love life.

“The NFL is laughing all the way to the bank,” she said on the “Got It From My Momma” podcast. “Good for them. They’re getting the ramifications of everything.”

Donna, who turned 71 on Monday, also appeared Friday on the “Today” show, where co-host Craig Melvin begged her to reveal what she discussed with Swift at the games.

“I’ll never tell you!” Donna said while the four co-anchors laughed.

Though she did admit to answering a technical question Swift had, Donna did her best to maintain the couple’s privacy.

“I don’t like to talk about it. It’s just one of those things where everyone saw me,” she said. “I was in the boxes with her. It’s another thing that’s amped up my life.”
