Donald Trump's Net Worth On Paper Is $2.5 Billion, But He Says That Figure Is Wrong

Highlights

  • Donald Trump's net worth is estimated to be around $2.5 billion, but he claims it is a different amount entirely.
  • Trump has built his net worth through various means, including paid appearances, property development, and private business holdings.
  • Trump asserts that his net worth fluctuates based on market conditions and his own and others' attitudes and feelings.

Donald Trump is a man who fascinates many people. There are plenty of things that he has said over the years that have sparked headlines, both in the traditional media and on social media, and whether they like him or not, no one can deny that he has managed to make a name for himself.

One thing he seems to be pretty clear about is his net worth. According to Forbes Magazine, his net worth is somewhere around the $2.5 billion mark. However, Trump doesn't accept this and has been claiming that his net worth is a different amount entirely.

How Did Donald Trump Build Up His Net Worth?

As a businessman who grew up surrounded by other businessmen, it was inevitable that Trump would end up going into the world of business in one way or another. And now having a net worth in the billions, there would have to be numerous ways that he has managed to build this net worth up.

One of these ways was getting paid $1 million for a Pizza Hut commercial, and he has also been on TV in other circumstances, being on The Apprentice for over a decade (earning $427 million in the process) and having small roles in Home Alone 2 and The Fresh Prince Of Bel-Air.

Other ways he has been able to amass his fortune are through public speaking, property development, having private business holdings, and also from a loan from his father that helped him build up his businesses.

What Does Donald Trump Say His Net Worth Is?

It's very difficult for anyone to hide what their real net worth is these days as they can claim it to be anything and someone could do a quick internet search and tell you that you've got it wrong. However, Trump has found a way around this.

Whilst he hasn't explicitly said what he believes his net worth to be, he does like to point out to people that there are a variety of factors to consider when it comes to calculating his net worth. Not just calculating his net worth every year, or even every few months, but every day.

Trump has previously claimed that his net worth changes based on the state of the market (which is true for everyone), but he also maintains that it changes according to both his and other people's attitudes and feelings.

This claim was first made to lawyers back in 2007 when Trump was in the middle of a deposition case after it was claimed in a book that Trump was only worth somewhere between $150,000 and $250,000.

Trump then went into further detail with his claim that the value of his net worth depends on how he is feeling, saying that his feelings on where the world is and where the world is going can change on a daily basis and that this, in turn, changes his net worth on a daily basis.

This isn't the first time he's said something like this, as all the way back in 1987, Trump appeared on The Late Show with David Letterman, where the interview seemed to mostly be about whether Trump actually knows his net worth.

David was very clear that he wanted Trump to spill the beans on his net worth, but Trump was determined to keep tight-lipped on the situation. There were many times throughout the interview that Trump tried to deflect the conversation onto something else entirely, but David was determined not to let the conversation slide.

Throughout the interview, Trump kept on saying that he had absolutely no idea what his net worth was. But when David asked Trump if he was worth a billion dollars, Trump replied "perhaps" and refused to say anything else.

However, there have also previously been rumors that Trump could end up broke one day, particularly as he was reported to be broke before and one year he even managed to lose $600 million in that time, which took him all the way down to the 429th richest person in America.

How Does Donald Trump Spend His Net Worth?

Despite having taken a massive hit to his net worth, Trump is still a billionaire, which means he should have plenty of wriggle room to spend that fortune on whatever he requires.
