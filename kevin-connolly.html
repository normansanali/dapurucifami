Kevin Connolly News - Us Weekly

Name:Kevin Connolly

Hometown:Patchogue, NY

Birthday:March 5, 1974

About

Born on March 5, 1974, New York-born Kevin Connolly is best known for playing Eric on HBO’s Entourage. Aside from his hit performance on the funny and sexy sitcom, the actor/director has appeared in various films including The Notebook, Antwone Fisher, Rocky V and He’s Just Not That Into You.

He also had a recurring role in the WB comedy Unhappily Ever After. His feature directorial debut, Gardner of Eden, was released in 2007.

The actor has been romantically linked to heiress Nicky Hilton, actresses Nikki Cox and Haylie Duff, and Dancing With the Stars‘ Julianne Hough.
