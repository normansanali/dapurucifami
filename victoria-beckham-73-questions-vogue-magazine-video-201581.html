Victoria Beckham Answers 73 Questions With Vogue: Video

Who is this lady?  

Victoria Beckham is the latest A-lister up for bat in Vogue’s popular “73 Questions” video series, and through a round of rapid-fire questioning at her newly opened retail store in London, the British fashion mogul, 40, unloads all types of juicy tidbits. To sum up the five-minute viewing experience: Mind blown.

Beckham enters the brightly serene store, wearing a sharp cream trench coat and pantsuit ensemble and greets the Vogue associate. See rare photos of Victoria smiling.

The two share an awkward hug — but that is to be expected from a woman who reveals why she never smiles. “I’m smiling on the inside,” the mom of four with hottie husband David Beckham said of her decision to never smile. “I feel that I have a responsibility to the fashion community.”
