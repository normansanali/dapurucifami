How did Jojo Fletcher start her Professional Career?

In 2013, Jojo Fletcher made her television debut when she and her half-brother Ben Patton featured in the first episode of the reality TV series “Ready for Love.”

Fletcher gained her initial notoriety in 2016 when she participated in the 20th season of Jojo Fletcher’s “The Bachelor,” which starred Ben Higgins. After Huggins selected Lauren Bushnell over her, she came in second place at the end of the competition.

On March 14, 2016, Fletcher was revealed as the 12th season Bachelorette on “After the Final Rose.” As the main character in the program that she and her current fiancé, Jordan Rodgers, ended, she received a lot of attention.

Fletcher made an appearance in one “The Bachelor Winter Games” episode as well. As a new home improvement loan ambassador, Fletcher began working with Marcus at Goldman Sachs in April 2018.

2018 saw the debut of her clothing brand, Fletch. Fletcher and her fiancé Rodgers hosted the CNBC reality series “Cash Pad” in July 2019. The pair hosted “Battle of the Fittest Couples” on Paramount Network that same year.
