Adekunle Gold Gifts Wife, Simi A Brand New Car

Renowned Nigerian singer and entertainer, Adekunle Almoruf Kosoko, known within the showbiz circles as Adekunle Gold has acquired a brand new car for his wife, Simisola Bolatito Kosoko also known as Simi.

The award winning songstress, producer and performing artiste, Simi has taken to her official social media page to share a short video of the a car in motion on tagging her husband, Adekunle Gold in the caption.

The highly rated Nigerian musician, Adekunle Gold got his wife the brand new car ahead of his Australia tour. Simi, who was unable to contain her joy also released a picture and a video of the vehicle’s interior.
