Zo Kravitz References Lisa Bonet

Media Platforms Design Team

Zoë Kravitz bore a striking resemblance to her mother, Lisa Bonet—and gave us an immediate flashback to Bonet's stint on "The Cosby Show"—when she showed up to the Coldplay concert in Los Angeles this week with a head of braids past her waist. We're all about embracing late-'80s/early-'90s carefree beauty, and what better inspiration than your own mom? There was no one chicer than Denise Huxtable after all.
