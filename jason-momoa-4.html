Jason Momoa known as Joseph Jason Namakaeha Momoa is an American actor and producer. As of 2023, Jason Momoa’s net worth is $25 million. He began his acting career in 1999 with Baywatch: Hawaii. It was a syndicated action-drama series, and he had to play the role of Jason Loane.

The series ended in 2001. After that, in the year 2005, he was seen on Stargate Atlantis, the Syfy science fiction series. He played the character of Ronon Dex, and the series ended in 2009.

He was also seen on the famous series Game of Thrones, the HBO fantasy drama series, as Khal Drogo in the first two seasons. His character was seen from 2011 to 2012. Later, in the year 2016, he was seen on Frontier, the Discovery Channel historical drama series, as Declan Harp.

This series came to an end in 2018. Next, he made an appearance in See, the Apple TV+ science fiction series and portrayed the role of Baba Voss. This series is still being aired. He is seen as the lead role in two of his latest series.

Not just television series, since 2016, Momoa is also seen as portraying the role of Arthur Curry in Aquaman, which is the DC Extended Universe (DCEU).

The news that his role in Aquaman and the Lost Kingdom will reprise in 2022 was a big highlight in the year 2018. He will also make appearances in film in the year 2021, which is an adaptation of the science fiction novel Dune. Here he will be seen playing the role of Duncan Idaho.

Jason Momoa Wiki/Biography

Born on 1 August 1979, Jason Momoa’s age is 43 Years Old as of 2023. He was born in Nanakuli, Honolulu, Hawaii. He was raised in Norwalk, Iowa, by Coni, his mother. He has a mixed descent because his father is of partial Native Hawaiian ancestry, whereas his mother is of German, Irish, and Native American descent. He is an American by nationality. He follows the Buddhist religion.
