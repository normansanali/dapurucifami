Plus-Size Model Ashley Graham Goes Nude in Glamour Iceland: Photos

Bare — and beautiful! Plus-size champion and Sports Illustrated swimsuit model Ashley Graham stripped down buck naked alongside her fellow models in a new spread for Glamour Iceland.

The issue, which was released on Friday, May 8, features ALDA models Graham, Marquita Pring, Julie Henderson, Inga Eiriksdottir, and Danielle Redman in the nude with the mission of transforming how beauty is perceived in the modeling world. ALDA, which means “wave” in Icelandic, is a coalition of models that looks to promote the ideal that beauty in no way is restricted to shape or size.

Eiriksdottir spoke in detail about their efforts. “We met secretly for a few months, came up with a business plan, and then met with all the major agencies in New York,” the model explained. “Most of them had never represented models bigger then a U.S. size 6, so we didn’t know how they would react. It was amazing to see the great response we got. After some negotiations, we decided to join IMG, as we felt they could help us have good influence in the business. They love what we stand for. It should’t matter if you are a size 2 or 12. Every model should be represented equally.”
