CM Punk Compares The Rock &amp; Roman Reigns to The Kardashians

CM Punk is out of action due to triceps injury but that doesn’t mean he can’t have poke fun at ongoing storylines in WWE. Which is why he recently decided to compare The Rock and Roman Reigns to The Kardashians.

CM Punk joined the WrestleMania XL Kickoff Press Conference with Michael Cole, Pat McAfee, and Big E. He even expressed his desire to witness Cody Rhodes punching The Rock in the mouth, getting a stern glance from The Rock in the process.

After showcasing his two weeks journey following triceps surgery, CM Punk decided to have some fun at the expense of The Rock, Roman Reigns and Cody Rhodes recently.

CM Punk took to his Instagram story and uploaded photo featuring Cody Rhodes, another one with The Rock and Roman Reigns and finally one featuring Khloe Kardashian with the WWE Golden Title, as he compared both of their families to The Kardashians.

“Wrestling has more than one Royal family.”

CM Punk’s abandoned WWE plans before his injury were also revealed. We will have to wait and see what WWE will have planned for Punk once he makes his triumphant in-ring return.
