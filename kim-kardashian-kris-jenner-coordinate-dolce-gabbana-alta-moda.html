Kim Kardashian and Kris Jenner Coordinated in the Most Luxurious Mother-Daughter Looks Ever

Kim Kardashian and mom Kris Jenner made a splashy entrance at Dolce & Gabbana’s Alta Moda show in Italy last night.

The mother-and-daughter duo wore glamorous back-to-back outfits, coordinating in luxe looks for the runway show and the subsequent celeb-studded after-party.

For her first appearance of the evening, Kardashian wowed in a deep plum gown with a draping off-the-shoulder detail, a thigh-high center leg slit, and a dramatically ruffled train. She paired the sophisticated piece with sheer mesh opera gloves and sparkling diamond jewelry, including a statement necklace, a large ring, stud earrings, and an anklet. To finish off the look, she stepped out in clear PVC pumps.

Meanwhile, family matriarch Jenner wore a body-hugging gown in an inky black hue, which she layered underneath a dark orange tiger-print robe with black feather boa trim along the center and sleeves. She accessorized with diamond stud earrings and a bedazzled pink cross necklace, plus a pair of strappy black heels.

Later in the evening, the duo changed into coordinating all-black outfits.

The Skims mogul transformed into a gothic bride via a black corseted maxi dress with gold accent details on the bodice and a black lace-trim cathedral veil. She accessorized with black mesh gloves and a ruby beaded necklace with a large gold sun pendant.

Jenner also went the lacy route, wearing a see-through lace wrap robe with billowing sleeves and a plunging neckline. Underneath, she wore a matching black playsuit. She capped off the look with a mini black purse.
