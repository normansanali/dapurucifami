Nate Diaz family, wife, children, parents, siblings

Nathan Donald Diaz better known as Nate Diaz is an American professional mixed martial artist, currently signed with the Ultimate Fighting Championship. Before signing with the UFC, Diaz competed in World Extreme Cagefighting, Strikeforce, and Pancrase. He has been in the UFC since winning The Ultimate Fighter 5.

Nate and his wife Misty have been blessed with two children. Their first daughter is called Nikayla Diaz. Nikayla was born on June 20, 2018, and currently, she is 4 years old. The second born is a newly born baby whose name is not known for now.
