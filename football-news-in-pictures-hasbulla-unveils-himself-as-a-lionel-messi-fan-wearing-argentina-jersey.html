In Pictures: Hasbulla unveils himself as a Lionel Messi fan wearing Argentina jersey

Hasbulla, a Russian influencer with a huge following on social media, is currently in Argentina. He’s been posting pictures and videos of himself having all the fun. In one of the videos, Hasbulla even wears a Lionel Messi shirt.

Hasbulla Magomedov traveled to Argentina for a presentation on Monday at the Gran Rex theater in Buenos Aires. But first, Argentine television will be visited by this adorable, infantile creature that is the subject of innumerable memes. The Instagram user, also known as Mini Khabib, will be entering a television studio for the first time in our nation.

Hasbulla announced the presentation on his social media accounts, and it will take place at the Gran Rex theater on the same day. He shared a post captioned “I’m coming to visit you, Argentina”  and the formal notification of his presentation at the historic coliseum on Corrientes Street on his Twitter account.

According to Lionel Messi, Argentina is not afraid of any team in the FIFA World Cup

Lionel Messi, the captain of Argentina, declared that his country has no fear of any opponent in the FIFA World Cup 2022, which will begin in Qatar the following month. The team was performing well, and the soccer GOAT predicted that they would bring the trophy back to their homeland in a recent interview.

The Copa America champions, unbeaten since 2019, will compete in the World Cup in 2022. The longest active unbeaten streak and one of the longest is held by Argentina, who have gone 35 games without a loss.

The Argentinian captain claimed that they were confident, brave, and hungry to compete against the best. On November 22, Argentina will play Saudi Arabia in the tournament’s opening game. Lionel Messi, who has competed in four World Cups to far (2006, 2010, 2014, and 2018), will play in his final World Cup in 2022. In 2014, the greatest of all time guided his country to the finals, where they were defeated by Germany. In 2018, France, the world cup champion, eliminated Argentina in the round of 16.
