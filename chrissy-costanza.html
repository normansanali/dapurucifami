Chrissy Costanza is the lead vocalist of the breakout band, Against the Current, which first gained a massive fan following on YouTube by uploading covers of various hit singles. They were signed by a record label in 2014 which initiated the creation of original songs by the band. She is also very active on social media and uses the platform to profess her love for food, video games, make-up, and skincare.
