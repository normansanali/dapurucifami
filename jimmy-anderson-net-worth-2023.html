Jimmy Anderson Net Worth 2023

Jimmy Anderson Net Worth 2023: Born on July 30, 1982, Jimmy Anderson, officially known as James Michael Anderson is an English international cricketer.

He developed a passion for playing cricket at a younger age and has been consistent throughout his career rising through the ranks to become one of the most sought-after cricketers.

At the time of this writing (Wednesday, June 21, 2023), he plays for the England Test cricket team, and previously played for England’s limited-overs cricket teams.

In domestic cricket, he represents Lancashire County Cricket Club. Anderson was a member of the England team that won the 2010 ICC World Twenty20.

Anderson plays as a right-arm fast-medium bowler and is regarded as one of the greatest swing bowlers in the history of cricket, and has taken over 1100 first-class wickets.

As of February 2023, he’s ranked as the number one Test bowler in the world in the International Cricket Council (ICC) Men’s Player Rankings

Among fast bowlers, he’s the leading Test wicket-taker of all time, being the only fast bowler to have taken 600 or more Test wickets, and is England’s record Test wicket-taker.

Anderson made his Test debut in 2003 and has played the most Test matches for England, and the second-most of any cricketer, behind Sachin Tendulkar.

He’s also England’s highest wicket-taker in ODIs with 269. As a batsman, he and Joe Root hold the world record for the highest tenth-wicket stand in Tests; 198
