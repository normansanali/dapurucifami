Chelsea Handler Explains Her Raunchy &quot;Sex Tape&quot;

When is a sex tape not a sex tape?

When it's a "joke," says Chelsea Handler. On Wednesday, RadarOnline reported that it had acquired a decade-old tape of comedian Handler, now 34.

In the tape, the future Chelsea Lately hostess allegedly appears in her apartment, does a typical stand-up routine before taking off all of her clothes and "engaging in an explicit sex act" with a British-accented male partner, Radar reports.

A rep for Handler tells UsMagazine.com: "It isn't a sex tape. It's a tape that was done for a stand up comedy bit."

And on her show Wednesday night, Handler didn't deny the existence of the tape or dispute its X-rated contents.
