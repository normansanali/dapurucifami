Thembi Nyandeni Biography, Age, Career &amp; Net Worth

Thembi Nyandeni (born 19 March 1958) is a South African actress best known for her role as Selina Khumba in the SABC1 drama series Zone 14, since 2005.

Thembi Nyandeni Age

She was born on the 19th of March 1958 in South Africa. As of 2021, she is 63 years old.

Career

She also has a starring role in the Mzansi Magic telenovela-turned-soap-opera isiBaya as Mkabayi Zungu, the family’s tough-talking matriarch, and Mpiyakhe’s niece.

In March 2014, she received the Royalty Soapie Awards’ inaugural Outstanding Lead Actress award for her performance in isiBaya.

Net Worth

She has an estimated net worth of $200,000 which she has earned as an actress.
