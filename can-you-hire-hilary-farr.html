Can you hire Hilary Farr?

Are you irritated with your home or desperate for an interior makeover? Well, Hilary Farr is your girl! However, if you want her to renovate your home, you're going to have to go on Love It or List It.

If you're willing to be on the television series, here are the qualifications you must possess, per HGTV. You must:

  • Currently own a home in the GTA [Greater Toronto Area] or the surrounding area.
  • Need expert design and construction help.
  • Have a minimum reno and design budget of at least $75,000.
  • Be willing to move out of your house for the duration of the renovation/shoot.
  • Be outgoing, fun, enthusiastic homeowners and available to be on camera for up to seven weekdays of filming (staggered over six to eight weeks).
