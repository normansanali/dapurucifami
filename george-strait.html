George Strait Net Worth, Age, Girlfriend, Family, Biography &amp; More

George Harvey Strait Sr., popularly known as George Strait is an American who is a popular singer, songwriter, actor, and music producer by profession. He is considered as “King of Country” by many of his fans and Music artist.

He is the one who has brought back the neotraditional country music in America. Texas Country, honky tank, western swing, and country are some of his music genres. For the album Troubadour, he has won the Grammy Awards.

George Strait Wiki/Biography

Born on 18 May 1952, George Strait’s age is 69 years as of 2022. He was born and raised in a well-settled Christian family from Poteet, Texas, the United States. He is an American by nationality and has a belief in the Christian religion.

He completed his early schooling at Pearsall High School, the United States.

After that, he enrolled himself at Texas State University where he completed his graduation. From the very beginning of his childhood, he was more interested in singing and various co-curricular activities rather than in studying.
