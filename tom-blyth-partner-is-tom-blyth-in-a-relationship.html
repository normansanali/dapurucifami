Tom Blyth Partner: Is Tom Blyth in a Relationship?

Tom Keir Blyth is a well-known English actor. As of this writing, Tom Blyth’s relationship status is unclear.

He keeps details of his personal life private.  Tom Blyth attended Arnold Hill Academy and then to Bilborough College.

Blyth’s career began with minor supporting appearances in the 2010 movie Robin Hood and Pelican Blood. In 2018, he co-starred in the coming-of-age film Scott and Sid with Richard Mason.

He was cast as Glen Byam Shaw in the Terence Davies-directed biographical drama film Benediction, which premiered in 2021 after graduating from Juilliard.

In 2022, Blyth appeared as a guest on HBO’s The Gilded Age and began portraying the eponymous character in the Epix series Billy the Kid.

He has upcoming film roles in the comedy Discussion Materials, which is based on Bill Keenan’s memoir of the same name, and The Hunger Games: The Ballad of Songbirds & Snakes, which is based on the same-titled prequel to The Hunger Games.
