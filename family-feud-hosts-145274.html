Family Feud Hosts: Steve Harvey, Louie Anderson, and More!

When you think of TV game shows, Family Feud is probably one of the first to come to mind. The long-running series remains so popular today after more than three decades on the air — it’s even still being regularly spoofed on Saturday Night Live!

Part of the competition's charm has always been its hilarious hosts, ranging from Seinfeld stars to TV’s most famous weatherman. That’s only continued with current host and busiest man in America, Steve Harvey, who also heads up the ever-popular spin-off, Celebrity Family Feud. More than 30 years after the show's 1976 premiere, it's clear this series isn't going anywhere. Scroll down for a refresher on all of the Family Feud hosts, past and present.
