Ivanka Trump Works Post-Baby Body in White Swimsuit: Photos

100 on that one-piece! Ivanka Trump modeled her post-baby curves in a classic swimsuit while jet-skiing with her husband, Jared Kushner, off the coast of Croatia, on Friday, August 12.

The VP at the Trump Organization (and trusted adviser to the Republican presidential candidate, her dad Donald Trump), 34, flaunted her hourglass shape in a white suit with scalloped edges. Though she gave birth to her son Theodore just this past March, the mom of three looked strong and slim in the piece.
