Hotel Grace Majestic, Madgaon, India

Welcome to Grace HospitalityGrace Hospitality is the creation of Mr. Altone D’costa’s hard work, dedication and determination to pioneer a brand of Hotels and Resorts in Goa.
Back in 2005, realizing the need for more Hotels in Goa to accommodate tourists, Mr. D’costa with Mrs. Valanki D’costa had ventured into the Hospitality Industry with their first Hotel in Margao – Hotel La Grace.
Over the years, their second establishment – La Grace Resort in Benaulim, South Goa featuring , Apartments and villas has been servicing Goa’s coastal tourists.
Their commitment and zeal is now resulting in the newest establishment in one of Goa’s major towns – Hotel Grace Majestic in Margao.
The experience of the stay in our hotels is nothing short of enjoyable and our guests know they can trust Grace Hospitality to deliver the perfect holiday experience!
