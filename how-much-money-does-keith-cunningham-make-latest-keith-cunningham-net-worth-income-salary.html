Keith Cunningham was born in Breaffy Castlebar, Co Mayo, on October 30, 1979. He is an Irish radio producer and presenter who has won numerous awards. He is currently employed by 100-102 Today FM, where he hosts ‘The KC Show’ (as of January 2010). He formerly worked for Cork radio station RedFM for 8 years, including 6 years as the host of The Red Rooster Show’s primary breakfast show. Cunningham worked at RedFM as an assistant programme director and produced multiple award-winning shows for the national networks of RedFM and Power FM in Turkey.
